SMS Messaging

By providing your mobile phone number and consenting to receive SMS messages, you agree to receive text messages from Adam Goodman. These messages may include appointment reminders, event notifications, follow-up communications, and other informational content.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a single confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. To resume messages, reply START.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message or contact agoodman@goodmans.com.

Your mobile phone number is used solely to send you the messages you have consented to receive. It is not sold, shared, or used for any other purpose.

No Third-Party Sharing

Your phone number and any related personal information will not be shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.
